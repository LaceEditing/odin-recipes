Mac & Cheese

Description

This macaroni and cheese recipe will completely knock your socks off! Eating it will literally cause you to become catatonic due to the pure overload of happiness and carbohydrates entering your system.

It's like a diabetic coma for your emotions!

Seriously though be careful.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Cheese
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Carefully take the macaroni out of the box
  2. Delicately boil the macaroni in a bowl of water for 15 minutes
  3. Melt the cheese, slowly and cautiously, until the macaroni is ready
  4. Drain the macaroni, making sure not to anger the macaroni overlord: Macaronius Charthisicus The Destroyer
  5. Mix the macaroni with the cheese :)
  6. Eat some bread to celebrate your victory
