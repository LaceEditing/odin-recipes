A Glass of Water

Description

Have you ever wanted all of the comforts of drinking water, without any of the hassle of having to find the local watering hole and lean your precious little lips down into the murky depths of the lake? Well now you don't have to!

Introducing a glass of water! This delicate little darling will satisfy all of your parched little needs, or your money back!

Ingredients

  • water
  • a glass

Steps

  1. Take the glass
  2. Pour the water into the glass
  3. you cannot escape the inevitable arrival of the final days of your life
  4. Drink up! :)
